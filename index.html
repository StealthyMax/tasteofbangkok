About Us

Taste of Bangkok is a Thai restaurant owned by Tida & Maksim Kurpichau. The restaurant was opened
in March of 2024, and has since then been operating in New York in four primary locations - Saint Albans,
Staten Island, Brooklyn, and Astoria. The restaurant serves quality Thai food for great prices and has
received many great reviews over the short time it has been open to the public.
